Our Story

Humba Logistics Pvt Ltd is a transport and logistics company based in Harare, Zimbabwe. We provide reliable, efficient, and affordable logistics solutions for individuals, small businesses, and the construction sector.

Founded by Nigel Kamungere, a Zimbabwean entrepreneur with a vision to build sustainable businesses in logistics, construction, and real estate, Humba Logistics is committed to delivering value, creating opportunities, and building a lasting legacy across Africa.

Our operations are built on a fleet-based system with structured driver management and local supervision, ensuring every delivery meets the highest standards of reliability and professionalism.
